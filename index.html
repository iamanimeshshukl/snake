#Welcome To Sarpmitra-Chitra

Hi I am Pujansh Srivastava

Welcome to my world of wildlife conservation and rescue. My name is Pujansh Srivastava, but many know me as Sarpmitra Chitra. Since 2018, I have dedicated myself to the rescue and protection of snakes and other reptiles in Unnao, Uttar Pradesh. What began as a personal passion has evolved into a mission to educate and protect both animals and the communities they inhabit.
